# 📊 Olist E-Commerce Business Analysis

End-to-End Analytics | Customer Segmentation | Delivery Performance | Business Insights  

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a full-cycle business analysis of the Brazilian E-Commerce Public Dataset by Olist.  
The objective is to extract actionable insights from sales, customer, product, and delivery data to support data-driven decision-making.

The analysis covers data cleaning, feature engineering, exploratory data analysis (EDA), customer segmentation using RFM modeling, operational performance evaluation, and business recommendations.

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

- Python  
- Pandas  
- NumPy  
- Matplotlib  
- Jupyter Notebook  
- Power BI (Interactive Dashboard)

---

## 🔎 Project Workflow

### 1️⃣ Data Cleaning
- Merged multiple datasets (orders, customers, products, payments, reviews, sellers)
- Handled missing values.
- Corrected data types.

### 2️⃣ Feature Engineering
- Identified late deliveries
- Generated monthly and yearly sales features
- Built repeat customer indicators
- Developed RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) metrics

### 3️⃣ Sales Analysis
- Monthly sales trend (2016–2018)
- Revenue by product category
- Top 10 sellers by revenue

### 4️⃣ Customer Segmentation (RFM)
Customers were segmented into:
- Platinum
- Loyal Customers
- Potential
- At Risk
- Lost Customers

A simplified segmentation was also created:
- High Value
- Potential
- Low Value / At Risk

### 5️⃣ Delivery Performance Analysis
- Average delivery time: **12.02 days**
- Late delivery rate: **7.66%**
- Strong relationship between late deliveries and 1-star reviews

### 6️⃣ Customer Behavior Insights
- ~96% of customers are one-time buyers
- Need to improve retention strategies

---

## 📈 Key Business Insights

- Sales show steady growth from 2016 to 2018.
- Revenue is concentrated among a smaller group of high-value customers.
- Customer retention is a major opportunity area.
- Late deliveries significantly impact customer satisfaction.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Implement loyalty programs to increase repeat purchases.
- Improve delivery monitoring.
- Launch targeted campaigns for high-value customers.
- Re-engage at-risk customers through promotions and incentives.
- Monitor seller performance to maintain service quality.
